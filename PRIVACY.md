# Privacy Policy

**Chat Auto Exporter** runs entirely on your local machine. It does not collect, transmit, or share any data.

## What the plugin does

When a Claude Code session ends, is compacted, or is cleared, the plugin reads the local conversation transcript and writes a text file to your project's `.claude/chat-exports/` directory.

## What stays local

- Conversation content
- Tool usage and command output
- Exported `.txt` files

Nothing leaves your machine. There are no analytics, no remote servers, and no third-party services involved.
